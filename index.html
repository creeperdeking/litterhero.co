Our mission

Living in a clean and safe town is something every american aspire to. But when we look at our streets, we often find them riddeled with litter, and the city government often have difficulties cleaning and fixing our cities efficiently.

We here at litterhero allow conciencious citizens report trash, litter, needles, plotholes and graffities in a matter of seconds though our app by simply taking and tagging a picture.

We then provide municipalities with tools to help them monitor the issues and organise their teams to fix them efficiently and quickly.

A word from Kenneth, founder:

"We aim at making issue reporting painless, to help connect citiy employees and conciencious citizens. Our product considerably ease the work of city workers, give them a map of all reported litter in the city, and thelp them organize their cleaning operation, as well as giving a transparent account of their service to the town. LitterHero makes modern city service managing tools availlable even for medium to small cities."
